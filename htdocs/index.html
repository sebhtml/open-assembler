Improved high-throughput sequencing data assembly.
Sébastien Boisvert, François Laviolette, Frédéric Raymond, Mario Marchand, and Jacques Corbeil.
Submitted to PNAS.


Examples

454:

DNA.rb file1.sff file2.sff file3.sff


Solexa:

DNA.rb file1.fastq file2.fastq file3.fastq


Sanger:

DNA.rb file1.fasta file2.fasta file3.fasta


True hybrid assembly:

DNA.rb file1.sff file2.fastq file3.fasta

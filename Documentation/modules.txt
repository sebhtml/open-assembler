DNA.rb is the main script.

It calls these modules:



dna_ConvertSffToFasta  

dna_BuildGraph         

dna_ExtractContigs     

dna_MergeContigs       

dna_KeepLargeContigs

dna_JoinContigs

dna_ScaffoldContigs   

dna_ConvertFastaToAfg  

dna_ValidateContigs    



454 example:

DNA.rb file.sff


Solexa example:

DNA.rb file.fastq


Sanger example:

DNA.rb file.fasta


Mixed example:

DNA.rb file.sff file.fastq file.fasta
